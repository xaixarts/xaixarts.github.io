Proceedings of The Fourth International Workshop on eXplainable AI for the Arts (XAIxArts 4)

This fourth international workshop on explainable AI for the Arts (XAIxArts) brought together a community of researchers and creative practitioners in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Interaction Design, AI, explainable AI (XAI), and Digital Arts to explore the role of XAI for the Arts.

Workshop at the ACM Conference on Creativity and Cognition (C&C 2026). London, United Kingdom, 13th July 2026. Workshop website.
